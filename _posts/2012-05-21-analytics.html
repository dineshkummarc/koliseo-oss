Configuring your Tracker ID

You can feed into Google Analytics traffic data about your shows and venues inside Koliseo. To enable this feature just go to your profile page and click "Edit". In the following page, introduce your Tracker ID into the corresponding field:

If you don't have a Google Analytics account you may create one for free here.

After clicking save you should typically wait one day until the server gets significant data, but you can check that it has been properly configured using the real-time reports in Google Analytics.

Recorded data

After your Google Analytics ID has been successfully configured any visit to your shows, venues or user profile page will be recorded. You can choose how to take advantage of these statistics:

  • Offline ad campaigns (radio, TV) can be compared with the traffic flow to identify working patterns.
  • You can track online sources such as blogs and social networks linking to your show page.
  • You can relate pricing to changes in your conversion rate.
  • Analyze the shows that are getting most attention and identify why.
  • Get access to over 80 customized reports.
You can get more information at the Google Analytics Help Center or contact us to get these reports as a customized service.
