What is the cost of using Koliseo?

Koliseo is a free platform for event organizers. It operates on a fee charged over the ticket price that is paid with the ticket purchase. Everything else, including Koliseo Checkpoint Charlie for Android, is free.

What fee does Koliseo apply over the ticket price?

The fee is 7% of the ticket price, with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 15 of the event currency. The following is a conversion table for euros:
Ticket price Applicable fee
between 0.01 and 14 EUR1 EUR
between 15 and 214 EUR7% of the ticket price
above 215 EUR15 EUR

What about free events?

Free tickets are exempt of the minimum fee. If you are not making any income out of your event, we neither.

Which currencies does Koliseo support?

At the current time we support payment in USD (US dollars), EUR (euros) and GBP (British pounds). Money transfers are executed in the same currency of the event.

How do I receive payment for my sold tickets?

In order to receive payment you must first have introduced your payment data. On your user profile page click "Edit" and go to the "Payment data" tab.

Koliseo will allow you to introduce your event, but as soon as we have pending money transfers you will receive a reminder periodically to introduce your payment data.

When do I receive payment for my sold tickets?

Transfers are executed during the week after your performance takes place.
