Welcome to Koliseo Checkpoint Charlie

Koliseo Checkpoint Charlie is the free Android application that validates tickets for your events. To use this application you must have a working Koliseo account with programmed shows.

  1. The login process is the same used by the website.

  2. Once logged in, you can select your venue from the list or search using the text box.

  3. After selecting the venue, a list of dates with scheduled performances will be displayed. Selecting a date will display a menu with the available actions.

  4. From here you can display the list of scheduled performances by clicking the first button.

    To start validating tickets, just click the button in the middle. Your display should switch to camera mode, where you can scan tickets by displaying the QR code (the dotted square) centered inside the red horizontal line. If the code is not being recognized try putting it farther away.

    You can also introduce the ticket localizer manually using the third button.

While validating, the number of validated and rejected tickets is displayed at the bottom right corner of your screen. Even if you are using the Android validator, you may want to prepare a printed list of attendees to take with you, just in case.

Did you know? you can validate tickets printed on paper or directly from the screen of another cell phone.

Why the name?

Checkpoint Charlie was the crossing point between East Berlin and West Berlin during the Cold War. It was a cold night of March and we were lazily wondering a good name. We just reproduced the complex train of thought that made others arrive to the number 42.
